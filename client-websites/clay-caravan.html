Who we are

Clay should feel easy to start.

The Clay Caravan makes ceramics feel relaxed and easy to join in. We bring the studio setup to familiar places, then guide the group through the making part.

No stiff studio mood. No pressure to be good already. Just clay, time, and something you made yourself.
